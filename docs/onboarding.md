# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to the Team

Welcome to our company! This guide will walk you through the onboarding process for new employees.

## First Day

On your first day, you will:

1. Meet your manager and team members in a welcome meeting
2. Receive your laptop and equipment from IT (Room 301)
3. Complete HR paperwork including tax forms and benefits enrollment
4. Get your badge and building access set up at the security desk

## First Week

During your first week, please complete:

- **IT Setup**: Install required software (Slack, GitHub, VS Code, Docker)
- **Security Training**: Complete the mandatory cybersecurity awareness training on the LMS
- **Codebase Tour**: Your buddy will walk you through the main repositories
- **Architecture Overview**: Attend the recorded architecture session (link in Confluence)

## First Month Goals

By the end of your first month, you should:

- Have submitted at least one pull request
- Completed all mandatory training modules
- Had a 1:1 with your skip-level manager
- Set up your quarterly OKRs in the goals tracking system

## Key Contacts

- **HR**: hr@company.com
- **IT Support**: it-help@company.com (or #it-help on Slack)
- **Facilities**: facilities@company.com

## Tools and Access

All new engineers need access to:

- GitHub (request via IT ticket)
- AWS Console (request through your manager)
- Jira for project tracking
- Confluence for documentation
- Datadog for monitoring
