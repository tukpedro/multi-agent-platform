# Employee Benefits Guide

## Health Insurance

We offer comprehensive health coverage for all full-time employees:

- **Medical**: Choice of PPO or HMO plans through Blue Cross Blue Shield
- **Dental**: Delta Dental PPO plan, covering preventive and major care
- **Vision**: VSP plan with annual eye exam and frame allowance
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment
- Company covers 90% of premiums for employees, 70% for dependents

## Retirement

- 401(k) plan with company match up to 4% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- Investment options through Fidelity

## Time Off

- **PTO**: 20 days per year for all employees (accrued monthly)
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year
- **Holidays**: 11 paid company holidays
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks for secondary
- **Bereavement**: 5 days for immediate family

## Professional Development

- Annual learning budget of $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: up to 2 conferences per year (company-paid)
- Internal tech talks every Friday at 4 PM
- Tuition reimbursement for job-related courses (up to $5,000/year)

## Other Perks

- **Remote Work**: Hybrid model — 3 days in office, 2 remote (flexible)
- **Equipment**: $1,500 home office stipend for new employees
- **Wellness**: $50/month wellness reimbursement (gym, meditation apps, etc.)
- **Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax transit and parking benefits
- **Employee Referral Bonus**: $5,000 for successful engineering referrals

## Enrollment

Benefits enrollment happens:
- During onboarding (first 30 days)
- During annual open enrollment (November each year)
- Within 30 days of a qualifying life event

Contact hr@company.com for any benefits questions.
